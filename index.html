Summary

I love computer programming, graphics design, robotics, electronics, and embedded systems. Sometimes I write poems and short stories as a hobby. I have worked in many small startup companies as well as tried to build one. Apart from that, I tried to make a platform to teach mid to high school students computer programming, also to inspire and motivate them so that they can feel the beauty of programming.

Currently, I am doing my undergrad at BRAC University in Computer Science and Engineering as Major and Business Administration as my Minor. On that note I am doing CSE391: PROGRAMMING FOR THE INTERNET course [Piazza Page link] . Along with my studies, I have a part-time job as an Engineer (Level 1) at Datasoft Manufacturing & Assembly Inc. .

My personality type is ENTP [Extraversion (E), Intuition (N), Thinking (T), Perception (P)]. As a consequence, I am highly motivated, curious, active and spontaneous in behavior.
